## Architecture

The Shutter Transition is built for pure performance. Instead of animating `height` or `width` (which triggers expensive browser reflows), it relies entirely on hardware-accelerated CSS transforms.

When the transition triggers:
1. Two `div` elements (representing the two halves of the shutter) are mounted.
2. If `direction="vertical"`, the top panel animates from `translateY(-100%)` to `0%`, and the bottom from `100%` to `0%`.
3. If `direction="horizontal"`, the left panel animates from `translateX(-100%)` to `0%`, and the right from `100%` to `0%`.

This ensures 60fps performance on all devices.
