---
title: Expandable Sidebar
description: A sleek, modern vertical navigation rail that fluidly expands to reveal full text labels.
category: Navigation
---

The `Expandable Sidebar` is a space-efficient navigation component. In its collapsed state, it acts as a minimalist icon rail. When interacted with, it expands fluidly using Framer Motion spring physics to reveal text labels, allowing users to navigate complex hierarchies without sacrificing permanent screen real estate.
