---
title: Tilt Card
description: A 3D interactive card that physically tilts based on cursor position using Framer Motion physics.
category: Cards
---

The `TiltCard` component creates a highly tactile 3D hover effect. As the user moves their cursor over the card, the card physically tilts towards (or away from) the cursor, complete with a subtle gloss overlay that reacts to the lighting angle. Now featuring **3 interactive depth variants**.
