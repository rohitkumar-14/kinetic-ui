## Overview
A lightweight SVG noise filter that creates a subtle, tactile film grain effect over your entire page or specific sections. This is one of the most common techniques used by award-winning websites to combat the sterile look of pure digital interfaces.

## Usage

```tsx
import { NoiseOverlay } from "@/components/creative/noise-overlay"
```

```tsx
<div className="relative w-full h-screen">
  <NoiseOverlay opacity={0.05} />
  {/* Content here */}
</div>
```
