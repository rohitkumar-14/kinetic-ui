title: Minimap

category: Layout
---

The `Minimap` component is a small, floating overview widget that gives users a bird's-eye view of a large virtual canvas. It renders colored rectangles for every item on the board and a white viewport indicator showing the currently visible area.

Users can **click or drag** anywhere inside the minimap to instantly teleport the main viewport to that region of the canvas.

It's designed to pair perfectly with our `DraggableCanvas`, `PhysicsCanvas`, or `ForceGraph` components, but it works with any scrollable or pannable surface since it uses a fully controlled props API.

> [!TIP]
> **Controlled Architecture:** The minimap is fully controlled via props. It does not manage its own scroll state. This makes it trivially composable with *any* canvas system—just wire up `viewportX`, `viewportY`, and `onViewportChange`.
