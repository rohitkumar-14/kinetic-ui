---
title: Interactive Fluid Background
description: A WebGL background that behaves like colorful smoke or liquid metal responding to mouse trails.
category: Background Effects
---

An advanced WebGL background shader built with `react-three-fiber` that simulates swirling liquid metal or colorful smoke. It calculates velocity from mouse movements to create beautiful interactive trails.
