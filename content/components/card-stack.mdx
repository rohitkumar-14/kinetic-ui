title: Card Stack

category: Cards

The `CardStack` component is perfect for testimonials, product features, or any series of items you want to present compactly. Users can grab and drag the top card to flick it to the back of the deck. Now fully updated with **4 layout variants** to control how the cards stack and fan out.

## Performance Note

To ensure buttery-smooth Framer Motion dragging physics, the component automatically clips rendering to only show the **top 3 cards** in the DOM at any given time, regardless of how many items are passed in the array. This keeps the DOM lightweight.
