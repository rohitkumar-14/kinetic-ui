---
title: Text Mask Video Reveal
description: A massive typography mask that reveals a video as it scales up exponentially on scroll.
category: Interaction Physics
---

The TextMaskVideoReveal uses CSS mask-image combined with Framer Motion scroll tracking to punch a literal hole through your typography into a background video layer.
