The `GlassTabs` component is an ultra-premium variation of standard navigation tabs. 

Instead of a solid color block for the active tab indicator, it uses a heavily styled, frosted glass element (`backdrop-blur-md` with inner white borders and shadows). Powered by Framer Motion's `layoutId`, the glass block physically stretches, squishes, and morphs across the tab buttons when clicked.

## Live Preview

> [!TIP]
> **Refraction Effect:** Notice how the active tab indicator isn't just gray—it actually refracts and blurs the vibrant rotating orbs behind it! This effect only works if your app has a colorful, dynamic background behind the tabs.
