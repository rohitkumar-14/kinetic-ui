---
title: Physics Canvas
description: A whiteboard wrapper that brings real 2D physics to standard DOM elements.
category: Layout
---

The `PhysicsCanvas` uses the `matter.js` engine to turn a standard web layout into a physical sandbox. By wrapping your elements in a `PhysicsItem`, they are automatically registered as rigid bodies with real mass, friction, and bounce. 

Users can grab, drag, and throw these DOM elements around the screen naturally, and they will collide with each other and the invisible boundaries of the canvas.

> [!TIP]
> **Pointer Events:** If your `PhysicsItem` contains images or text that might interfere with the dragging action, make sure to add `className="pointer-events-none"` to those child elements so the drag event registers cleanly on the wrapper!
