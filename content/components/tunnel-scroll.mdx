---
title: Tunnel Scroll
description: A 3D scrollytelling wrapper that turns standard HTML into floating billboards inside a WebGL tunnel.
category: Scrollytelling
---

The `TunnelScroll` component acts as an immersive page wrapper. Instead of the page scrolling down normally, the component maps your scroll progress to the Z-axis of a 3D camera. 

It wraps your standard React `children` inside `@react-three/drei`'s `<Html>` projection, rendering them as standard DOM elements that live physically inside the 3D space of the tunnel!

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Fixed Widths:** Because the children are projected into 3D space, standard CSS responsive behaviors (like `w-full` expanding infinitely) can break the projection and cause the elements to size incorrectly. Always provide fixed maximum widths (e.g., `w-[800px]` or `w-[1000px]`) to the top-level children you pass into the wrapper.
