title: Text Reveal
description: A scroll-triggered staggered text animation that reveals words sequentially.
category: Text Effects

The `TextReveal` component breaks a string down into individual words and orchestrates a highly customizable entrance sequence when the text scrolls into the viewport. Now updated to use **Framer Motion** (removing the GSAP dependency) with **4 distinct built-in variants**.

## Viewport Logic

The component utilizes Framer Motion's `useInView` hook with `margin: "-15% 0px"`. This ensures the stagger animation reliably triggers exactly once when the heading scrolls far enough into the viewport to be comfortably seen by the user.
