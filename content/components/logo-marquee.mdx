category: Text & Typography

A highly polished, infinitely looping marquee component built with Framer Motion. It seamlessly supports horizontal and vertical scrolling, adjustable speeds, pausing on hover, and custom gradient masks. Perfect for showcasing trusted partners, tech stacks, or features.
