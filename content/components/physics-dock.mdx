---
title: Physics Dock
description: A playful, magnetic dock navigation component driven by real 2D collision physics.
category: Navigation
---

The `PhysicsDock` takes a standard navigation pattern and injects life into it. Icons act as physical bodies connected via invisible springs. When you drag an item, it pulls on the spring and realistically collides with and pushes away other items. 

Powered by `matter-js` for the physics engine and `framer-motion` for ultra-smooth 60fps rendering.
