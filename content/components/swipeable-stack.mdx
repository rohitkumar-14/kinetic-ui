---
title: Swipeable Stack Deck
description: A stack of physical cards that users can drag and swipe away using advanced gestures.
category: Cards
---

Bring Tinder-style swiping mechanics to your web application. Using Framer Motion's `drag` API and advanced physics, users can physically grab the top card of a stack and throw it off the screen. As cards are removed, the cards underneath perfectly scale and slide up to take their place.
