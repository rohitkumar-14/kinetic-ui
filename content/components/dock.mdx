---
title: Dock
description: A fluid, interactive macOS-style dock navigation that dynamically magnifies items on hover.
category: Navigation
---

The `Dock` component brings the classic macOS navigation experience to the web. Built entirely with Framer Motion's `useMotionValue` and `useTransform` hooks, it tracks the user's cursor distance to smoothly scale up items and gently push adjacent items away. It's perfect for fixed global navigation or sticky sub-menus.
