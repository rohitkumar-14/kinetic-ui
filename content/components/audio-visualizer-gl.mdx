---
title: Audio Reactive Visualizer
description: A WebGL shader mesh that syncs geometric distortion and color tinting to live audio frequencies.
category: Shader Physics
---

The AudioReactiveVisualizer leverages the Web Audio API to stream frequency data directly into a custom GLSL shader via uniforms, distorting a 3D Icosahedron in real-time to the bass and treble.
