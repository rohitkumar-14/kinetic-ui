---
title: Cinematic Preloader
description: A global, full-screen loading sequence that slices open perfectly to reveal the page.
category: Page Transitions
---

The ultimate first impression for any Awwwards-style website. This component acts as a `z-[9999]` fixed overlay that runs a beautiful percentage counter. Once loaded, the screen physically splits in half horizontally, sliding apart seamlessly to reveal your application.
