---
title: Flashlight Reveal
description: A pitch-black section where your cursor acts as a physical spotlight to reveal the vibrant content underneath.
category: Mouse Interactions
---

An incredibly visual discovery mechanic. Built using CSS `mask-image` and Framer Motion's high-performance `useMotionTemplate`, this component overlays a pitch-black layer on top of a vibrant gradient layer, dynamically punching a perfect spotlight hole exactly where your cursor is hovering.
