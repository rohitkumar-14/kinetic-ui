## Architecture

This component is built on top of **React Three Fiber** and **Drei**. It leverages `<PresentationControls>` to allow users to intuitively click and drag the 3D model around, which snaps back to its original position using spring physics.

We also use `<Environment>` to apply High Dynamic Range (HDR) lighting, instantly giving standard materials realistic reflections and metallic properties.
