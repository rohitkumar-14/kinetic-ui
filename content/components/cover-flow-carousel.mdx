---
title: Cover Flow Carousel
description: A 3D draggable carousel that rotates and scales items dynamically based on their distance from the center.
category: Layout & Data
---

A highly premium draggable carousel that creates a perfect 3D cylinder illusion without using WebGL. By dynamically hooking into Framer Motion's `x` offset, each card mathematically calculates its own `rotateY`, `scale`, and `filter` based on its physical distance from the center of your screen.
