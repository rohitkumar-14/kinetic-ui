---
title: Magnetic Button
description: A button that snaps and physically pulls towards the user's cursor on hover using spring physics.
category: Buttons
---

The `MagneticButton` uses Framer Motion's robust spring physics to create a highly tactile, physical interaction. When a user hovers near or over the button, it pulls towards their cursor, snapping back into place when they leave. Now featuring **3 physics variants** to simulate different physical materials.
