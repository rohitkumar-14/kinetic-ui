---
title: Horizontal Scroll Hijack
description: Pins a section and creates a horizontal scroll timeline before unlocking.
category: Interaction Physics
---

The HorizontalScrollHijack component uses Framer Motion's useScroll and useTransform to pin a vertical section in place while creating a massive horizontal timeline based on scroll velocity.
