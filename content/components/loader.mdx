---
title: Loader
description: Visual indicators used to express an unspecified wait time or display the status of an ongoing process.
category: Data Display
---

The `Loader` component gives users visual feedback during data fetching, form submissions, or while waiting for background operations to complete. It includes multiple design variants and scaling sizes.
