The `KineticTypography` component breaks down strings into individual `framer-motion` nodes. By applying mathematical transforms or physics springs to individual letters, it creates powerful "magnetic spline" waves, organic floats, and interactive hover effects. Now updated with **5 animation variants**.

## Variants

| Variant | Interaction | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `elastic` | Mouse Move | Maps cursor position to 3D rotation, scaling, and translation for a magnetic spline effect. |
| `wave` | Auto-playing | Continuous, staggered sine wave oscillation across the text. |
| `jump` | Hover | Individual letters spring upwards and change color when hovered. |
| `float` | Auto-playing | Letters drift organically with randomized delays, distances, and rotations. |
| `decode` | Mount & Hover | Scrambles letters through random characters before settling. Re-triggers on individual letter hover. |
