---
title: Horizontal Scroll
description: A creative scroll-linked animation component that translates vertical page scrolling into horizontal container movement.
category: Scroll Animations
---

The `HorizontalScroll` component is a high-impact layout technique often used in Awwwards-winning websites to showcase portfolios, image galleries, or features. It leverages Framer Motion's `useScroll` and `useTransform` hooks to hijack the vertical scroll and map it to a horizontal X-axis translation.
