---
title: Fluid Background
description: A stunning WebGL shader background that simulates fluid noise reacting to mouse movement.
category: Background Effects
---

The `FluidBackground` component uses React Three Fiber and a custom GLSL fragment shader. It generates multiple layers of Simplex 2D noise that drift over time and organically distort based on mouse proximity, creating a premium "liquid smoke" aesthetic perfect for hero sections.
