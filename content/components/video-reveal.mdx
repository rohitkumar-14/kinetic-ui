---
title: Video Reveal
description: Hover-triggered cinematic video reveal masks.
category: Media
---

A highly interactive media component that acts as a sleek typography container until hovered, at which point it elegantly reveals a looping video background using Framer Motion animations. Features multiple Awwwards-style reveal styles.
