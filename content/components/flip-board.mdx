---
title: Flip Board
description: A highly satisfying split-flap display component that animates characters with flip transitions, inspired by Vestaboard.
category: Text Effects
---

The `FlipBoard` component recreates the tactile, mechanical feel of a classic split-flap display. It rapidly cycles through a standard character set with realistic CSS 3D folding animations until it lands on your target text.
