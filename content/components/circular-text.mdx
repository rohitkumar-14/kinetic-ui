---
title: Circular Text
description: A 3D spinning ring of text that accelerates based on scroll velocity.
category: Text Effects
---

A highly engaging typographical component that wraps a string of text perfectly into a 3D circle. It spins continuously on its own, but integrates with Framer Motion's `useScroll` and `useVelocity` to physically accelerate the spin and tilt on the 3D axis when the user scrolls the page.
