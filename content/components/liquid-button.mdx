---
title: Liquid Button
description: A button that reveals a fluid, morphing liquid background layer on hover.
category: Buttons
---

The `LiquidButton` leverages SVG filters (`feGaussianBlur` and `feColorMatrix`) applied to a Framer Motion layer to create an incredibly organic, fluid animation effect when hovered. Now completely upgraded with **3 fluid interaction variants**.
