---
title: Interactive Particle Swarm
description: A highly performant 3D particle swarm shader that repels organically from the user cursor.
category: Shader Physics
---

The InteractiveParticleSwarm instantiates thousands of Three.js particles via a custom BufferGeometry. A vertex shader calculates real-time mouse repulsion using a distance field, while Additive Blending handles lighting.
