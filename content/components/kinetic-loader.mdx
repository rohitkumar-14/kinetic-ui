---
title: Kinetic Loader
description: A staggered text loading animation featuring 3D flips, waves, and bounces.
category: Text Effects
---

The `KineticLoader` component uses Framer Motion to split text into characters and animate them with a continuous stagger sequence. It's perfect for full-page loading screens or in-button loading states. Now updated to be **zero-dependency** (removed GSAP) and features **4 built-in animation variants**.
