title: Infinite Draggable Canvas
description: A massive 2D draggable surface perfect for Figma-style boards, nodes, or gallery layouts.
category: Layout
---

The `DraggableCanvas` provides a high-performance, frictionless dragging surface using Framer Motion. It acts as a massive container that users can grab and pan around, complete with elastic edge constraints and physical momentum. 

Perfect for creating spatial interfaces, node editors, or sprawling image galleries.
