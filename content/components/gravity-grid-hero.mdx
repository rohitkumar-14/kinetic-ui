---
title: Gravity Grid Hero
description: A highly interactive canvas-based hero section where background grid elements react dynamically to the user's cursor with spring physics.
category: Hero Components
---

The `GravityGridHero` renders a highly performant HTML5 `<canvas>` background underneath your hero content. The grid of dots (or connected lines) uses raw mathematics and spring physics to continuously calculate distance from the mouse pointer, repelling elements outwards before smoothly springing them back to their origin.
