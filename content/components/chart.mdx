A robust wrapper around the `recharts` library. Instead of hardcoding hex colors into your charts, this component automatically injects your Theme Customizer's CSS variables (like `--primary`) into the charts. This ensures your data visualizations always match your site's exact aesthetic, even when switching to dark mode.

## Architecture

The `ChartContainer` component provides a React Context that maps a `config` object containing labels and CSS colors down to the tooltip and the SVG charts. 

By defining colors as CSS variables internally, you can effortlessly swap out themes or toggle dark mode, and your recharts will instantly update without requiring a full React re-render.
