---
title: Magnetic Gallery
description: A grid of images that slightly rotate in 3D space to track the global mouse position.
category: Layout
---

The `MagneticGallery` component creates a stunning layout where every image tracks the global mouse vector. By calculating the distance from the mouse to the center of each image, we map that proximity to Framer Motion's `rotateX` and `rotateY` transforms, creating a localized magnetic "look at" effect.
