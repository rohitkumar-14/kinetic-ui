---
title: ASCII Renderer
description: A real-time engine that converts images, videos, and webcam feeds into interactive ASCII text.
category: Text Effects
---

The `AsciiRenderer` component is the ultimate "hacker" aesthetic tool. It works by painting an image, a playing video, or a live webcam feed onto a hidden HTML5 Canvas. It then reads the pixel data, calculates the perceived brightness of each pixel block, and maps it to a string of ASCII characters (from dark space ` ` to bright `@`).

It runs on a `requestAnimationFrame` loop, meaning it natively supports 60fps video and webcam playback directly in the DOM as raw text!
