---
title: Text Split Reveal
description: A massive headline that splits horizontally on hover to reveal hidden content.
category: Text Effects
---

An elegant layout technique where hovering over a large typographical element slices it horizontally using CSS `clip-path` and animates the two halves apart, revealing hidden content (like a call-to-action or video) in the newly created negative space.
