---
title: Horizontal Scroll Pin
description: A scrollytelling section that perfectly translates vertical scrolling into a horizontal gallery slider.
category: Scrollytelling
---

The quintessential Awwwards-winning effect. As the user scrolls vertically down the page, this component pins itself to the viewport and translates your scrolling into horizontal movement across a massive flex track, only unlocking vertical scroll when you reach the end.
