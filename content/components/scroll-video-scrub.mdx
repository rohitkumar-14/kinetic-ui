---
title: Scroll Video Scrub
description: A scrollytelling component where your scroll position perfectly controls the timeline playback of a video.
category: Scrollytelling
---

The ultimate Apple-style product reveal technique. This component pins a video to your screen and mathematically maps its `currentTime` to your scroll progress. Scroll down to play forward, scroll up to rewind in real-time.

> **Tip:** For production use, ensure your video is exported with keyframes on every single frame (e.g., using Handbrake or FFmpeg with `keyint=1`) to guarantee buttery smooth scrubbing, especially when scrolling backward!
