---
title: Parallax Depth Cards
description: A stack of cards that fly in with 3D rotation and stack up as you scroll.
category: Cards
---

A highly engaging layout component where cards don't just scroll normally, but instead fly in from the background, rotate in 3D space (`rotateX`), and stack on top of each other while fading out slightly to create a deep, parallax depth-of-field effect.
