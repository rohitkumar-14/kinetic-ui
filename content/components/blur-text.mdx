The `BlurText` component is a classic cinematic text reveal. By applying a heavy CSS `filter: blur()` and orchestrating a delayed stagger across the words or characters, it creates a feeling of a camera focusing in on the typography. Now updated with **4 distinct animation variants**.

## Viewport Logic

The component utilizes Framer Motion's `useInView` hook with `margin: "-10% 0px"`. This means the animation will automatically trigger exactly once when the text scrolls into the viewport (specifically, when it's 10% past the bottom edge of the screen), preventing animations from running off-screen.
