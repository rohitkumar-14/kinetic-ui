---
title: Fullscreen Menu
description: A bold, cinematic fullscreen navigation overlay with customizable entrance animations.
category: Navigation
---

The `FullscreenMenu` provides an immersive navigation experience, perfect for portfolios or creative agency sites. It overrides the entire viewport, forcing the user to focus purely on the navigation hierarchy. The underlying staggered text reveals give it a highly polished, premium feel. Now featuring **4 distinct entrance animations**.
