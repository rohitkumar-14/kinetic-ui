title: SVG Liquid Distortion
description: A native SVG filter wrapper that melts and distorts elements on hover.
category: Text Effects
---

The `SVGLiquidDistortion` component is an incredibly lightweight way to achieve WebGL-level shader effects (like water ripples, melting text, and heat haze) using only native DOM and `<svg>` filter primitives.

It renders an invisible `<svg>` block containing `<feTurbulence>` and `<feDisplacementMap>`, and uses `framer-motion` to animate the distortion scale and noise frequency when you hover over it. The effect is applied via the standard CSS `filter` property, meaning it works universally on text, buttons, and images!

## Installation

<CliInstallCommand componentName="svg-liquid-distortion" />

> [!TIP]
> **Padding & Clipping:** Because SVG filters operate on the exact bounding box of the element, extreme distortion can sometimes cause the pixels to clip at the edges. The component automatically wraps your children in a `<span>` with `p-2` (padding) to give the distortion room to bleed outward safely!
