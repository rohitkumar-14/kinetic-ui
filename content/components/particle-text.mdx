---
title: Particle Text
description: A massive headline that physically shatters into hundreds of physics-based particles when your mouse approaches.
category: Text Effects
---

An incredibly advanced WebGL-style effect built entirely using native HTML5 Canvas `ImageData`. It reads any text string, converts the pixels into physical particles, and applies repulsion and spring forces to them as the user's mouse interacts with the canvas.
