---
title: Spotlight Card
description: A card component that reveals a radial lighting gradient underneath as the cursor moves over it.
category: Cards
---

The `SpotlightCard` creates a beautiful dynamic lighting effect. Using a hidden radial gradient that perfectly tracks the mouse position, the lighting effect is only revealed when hovering over the card. Now equipped with **4 unique spotlight variants**, including animated pulsing.
