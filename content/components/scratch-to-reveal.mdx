---
title: Scratch to Reveal
description: A canvas component where the user scratches with their cursor to reveal hidden content.
category: Mouse Interactions
---

An interactive canvas component that uses `globalCompositeOperation = "destination-out"` to allow the user to "scratch" away a top layer and reveal hidden content underneath. Great for easter eggs or gamified interactions.
