---
title: Video Portal
description: Hover over links to reveal a fluid, liquid-shaped video preview portal that follows your cursor.
category: Mouse Interactions
---

A highly engaging list component perfect for portfolio sites or showcases. Hovering over a list item spawns a fluidly animating portal that smoothly tracks the cursor while playing a video preview of the hovered item.
