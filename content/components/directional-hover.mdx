title: Directional Hover
description: A smart card wrapper that detects mouse entry direction and animates an overlay with 5 unique effects.
category: Mouse Interactions
---

The `DirectionalHover` component tracks mouse events and calculates the angle of entry relative to the center of the bounding box. It then uses Framer Motion's `AnimatePresence` with custom variants to animate an overlay from the exact edge your cursor crossed — with **5 distinct animation effects**.

## Effects

| Effect | Animation | Feel |
|---|---|---|
| `slide` | Full slide from entry edge | Classic, snappy |
| `fade` | Opacity crossfade | Subtle, elegant |
| `scale` | Expand from entry edge | Curtain reveal |
| `flip` | 3D perspective rotation | Dramatic, playful |
| `blur` | Slide with gaussian blur dissolve | Dreamy, cinematic |

## How it Works

The component calculates the angle between the cursor and the card center using `Math.atan2`. It maps the result to one of four quadrants (top/right/bottom/left). That direction is passed as a `custom` prop to Framer Motion variants, which adapt the initial/exit animation accordingly. The `effect` prop selects which set of variants to use — from simple opacity fades to perspective-aware 3D flips.
