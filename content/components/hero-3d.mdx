The `Hero3D` component loads a Three.js canvas utilizing custom GLSL shader programs to perturb mesh geometry in real time. It reacts organically to mouse coordinates, deforming the glass-like torus-knot with sine/cosine offset frequencies.

## Performance Notes

> [!TIP]
> The Hero3D component relies on Three.js and `@react-three/fiber` which are heavy dependencies. Always import this component dynamically with `ssr: false` in Next.js to avoid blocking your initial page load.

WebGL shaders execute directly on the GPU. The torus knot is geometry-instanced with low vertex division counts to maintain 60fps rendering cycles across both mobile and integrated GPU devices.
