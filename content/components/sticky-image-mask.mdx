---
title: Sticky Image Mask
description: A cinematic scroll effect where a text mask infinitely scales to reveal background media.
category: Page Transitions
---

A cinematic scrollytelling section. As the user scrolls down, a massive text block acting as an SVG clipping mask infinitely scales up until the hidden image or video breaks out of the letterforms to cover the entire screen seamlessly.
