## How it works

This component encapsulates a `Canvas` from React Three Fiber. Inside the Canvas, it renders one of five geometry variants, each with its own `Scene` sub-component. During each frame, `useFrame` reads `state.pointer` (normalized mouse position) and smoothly interpolates the 3D object's rotation to follow the cursor. The `Float` helper from drei adds organic hovering motion. `Environment` provides realistic image-based lighting and `ContactShadows` grounds the object in the card.
