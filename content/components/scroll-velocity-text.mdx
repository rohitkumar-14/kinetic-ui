---
title: Scroll Velocity Text
description: Typography that dynamically stretches and skews based on your scroll speed.
category: Text Effects
---

A highly dynamic typographic component that uses Framer Motion's `useScroll` and `useVelocity` to calculate scroll speed in real-time, applying a dramatic stretch and skew effect that intensifies the faster you scroll.
