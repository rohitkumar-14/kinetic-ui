---
title: Team Section
description: A marketing block used to showcase your company's team members and leaders.
category: Marketing Sections
---

The `TeamSection` component is designed for "About Us" pages. It features multiple high-end layouts including minimal lists, grids, and glassmorphic interactive cards with integrated social links.
