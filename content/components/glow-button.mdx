---
title: Glow Button
description: A button featuring a dynamic radial gradient glow that follows the cursor position.
category: Buttons
---

The `GlowButton` creates a sleek, futuristic aesthetic by masking a moving radial gradient. As the user's cursor moves across the button, a subtle colored glow tracks their movement. Now fully updated with **3 distinct visual variants**.
