---
title: Refraction Cursor
description: A custom glassmorphism cursor that distorts and magnifies the background.
category: Mouse Interactions
---

A highly polished custom cursor that acts like a magnifying glass. It perfectly maps the background image inside the lens and applies a scale transformation, simulating a true physical glass refraction effect directly over your content.
